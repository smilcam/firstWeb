Crab Dribble

The Crab Dribble was made famous by Lebron James after he was upset to be called for a travel after shuffling his feet. The rule of traveling is always up for debate and the reason I think so is because superstars are almost never called for it. I think the league either needs to place a heavier emphasis on it or let all players take three steps. If Lebron James gets three sets than I think you should give it to Centers as well, if you let Dwight Howard have three steps in the post than you would see Centers start to dominate the game again.
